## Create an Account

1. Open Brunel Agent and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your **email address** and choose a **password**
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm your address
5. Return to Brunel Agent and log in

---

## Select or Create an Organization

After logging in, you'll always see the **Select Organization** screen — even if you only belong to one organization.

From here you can:

- **Create a new organization** — click **+ Create Organization**, enter a name, and click **Create**
- **Join via invitation** — if you were invited by a teammate, accept the invitation from your email before logging in. You'll appear directly in their organization
- **Select an existing organization** — if you belong to multiple organizations, choose which one to enter

<Note>
You'll see the organization selection screen on every login. This is by design — it supports teams where individuals work across multiple organizations.
</Note>

---

## Switching Organizations

To switch to a different organization, log out of Brunel Agent and select a different organization on your next login.
